Who We Are

We are a team from PATH, a global nonprofit committed to advancing health equity worldwide. PATH’s mission centers on transforming health systems through innovative technologies, public health research, and impactful partnerships.

Our Achievements and Legacy

Over the decades, PATH has played a pivotal role in reducing childhood mortality and infectious diseases. Key achievements include contributing to the development and distribution of affordable vaccines for diseases like meningitis, malaria, and rotavirus.

Why This Website Matters

This platform is a product of a collaborative effort between PATH and the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD). The goal of our partnership is to leverage OECD’s expertise in health system analysis and PATH’s field experience to present data-driven insights that can shape global vaccination policies.
